Who I am

I am Angelina McCann and I live in Abilene, Texas. I spent the majority of my work life as an executive office administrator for a major aerospace company. In 2021 the company made a shift and I found myself holding a layoff notice after 16 years of service.

After giving my new situation some serious consideration, I decided that it was time to do something that I want to do, rather than something I have to do. I have always had a creative side, so I decided it was time to put it to good use. I am currently a Web Design and Development student at Texas State Technical College, where I consistently maintain a 4.0 GPA. I am slated to graduate in May of 2023 with an Associate of Applied Science degree.

What I Do

I create websites that take your business to the next level. While I enjoy the technical aspect of web development, I find my passion is design, branding and marketing content. Let me help your company make a statement and ensure your customers find you with excellent SEO and social media marketing plans!

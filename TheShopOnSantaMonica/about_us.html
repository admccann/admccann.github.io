About Us

I'm Angelina McCann. I live in Abilene, Texas with my husband and our three spoiled dogs. We are parents to five sons, so you can imagine how I needed a "girly craft" space!

I inherited the creative gene from my mother, who had her own craft shop. We started The Shop on Santa Monica in 2018 and now there's no turning back! I love going into the craft room, spending hours making beautiful things, and now you can benefit from my inability to sit still! I refuse to design or sell anything that I wouldn't hang on my own front door or display in my home. You can depend on a high quality product from The Shop on Santa Monica; we want your home to feel warm and welcoming to your family and friends (and be the envy of your neighborhood!).

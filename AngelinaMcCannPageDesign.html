Design Brief

Project Goals Summary

Create a website with featured portfolio projects to increase website traffic, while offering customers a professional interactive experience and an easy to navigate website. Having a user friendly website will allow customers to view current projects and determine if Angelina is a good fit as a designer for their business or personal website need.


Target Audience Profile

The primary target audience is small business owners of all ages, middle to upper income. These business owners may, or may not have a brick and mortar location, have been in business for a maximum of five years, and feel that they have gotten to the point in their business that a website is a must. They are ready to start the e-commerce portion of their business. Secondary target audience members are those who want a personal website to keep family and friends updated regarding activities or events, or for fundraising purposes.


Perception/Tone/Guidelines

Angelina McCann web design should be perceived as a competent web designer that offers personal level design and branding services. She holds herself to the highest standard and will only present premium quality sites for her customers’ use.


Competitive Positioning

While there are a great many web designers currently in business, Angelina McCann prefers to specialize in small, new businesses that are just beginning to enter the world of internet marketing. Many designers focus on larger, more established companies and this is where Angelina comes in and helps smaller businesses really shine. Not only does she offer website design, but she also provides marketing strategies, branding and logo design. In the unlikely case of a customer with concerns, Angelina works with them personally until they are 100% satisfied and all issues are resolved.


Targeted Message

Advertising should focus on the predetermined demographic above, appealing to those who are middle upper to upper class with disposable income, and have a need for a website.  Include phrases such as “small business marketing” and “website design”, etc… should be utilized.
